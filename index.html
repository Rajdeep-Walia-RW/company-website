About

Cycrew is a cybersecurity company built for startups and SMEs. We offer expert consulting, auditing, SOCaaS and managed security services through our proprietary platform, CYBR‑X — a powerful solution for real-time threat detection, compliance, and visibility.

Our approach is simple: combine automation, AI, and human expertise to deliver enterprise-grade protection without enterprise-level complexity. Whether you’re securing a fast-growing startup or an established business, Cycrew is your trusted cybersecurity partner.

Our Mission

- To empower modern businesses with smart, accessible, and AI-driven cybersecurity solutions that scale with growth — without compromise.

We’re here to bridge the security gap for the businesses powering tomorrow’s economy. By combining human expertise, automation, and our CYBR‑X platform, we make cybersecurity simple, efficient, and proactive.

Our vision is to extend our secure-by-design philosophy across emerging markets - starting locally, scaling globally.
